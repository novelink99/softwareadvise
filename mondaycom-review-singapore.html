monday.com Review for Singapore SMEs

Updated 2026-05-06 · 12 min read · Singapore

Our Verdict

8.2/10

monday.com is an excellent work operating system for Singapore-based teams managing projects, campaigns, and operations. Strong automation, visual workflows, and integrations make it competitive, though pricing in SGD can be steep for early-stage SMEs. Best suited for growing teams of 5–50 people.

What Is monday.com?

monday.com is a cloud-based work operating system that helps teams plan, execute, and track projects and workflows. Unlike traditional project management tools, monday.com uses a flexible, no-code board interface that adapts to any team structure—whether you're managing marketing campaigns, product development, client services, or operational processes.

For Singapore-based SMEs, monday.com offers browser-based access without needing to worry about local server compliance or complex IT setups. The platform is designed for teams that want visibility, collaboration, and automation without technical complexity.

Pricing in Singapore (SGD)

monday.com pricing starts at approximately SGD 89/month for the Starter plan (annual billing). For growing SMEs, the Standard plan runs around SGD 189/month, and the Pro plan sits near SGD 389/month. All prices exclude GST at 9%, which applies to digital services purchased by Singapore entities.

When budgeting, remember that GST will be added to your invoice, pushing the actual monthly cost higher. For example, a Standard plan at SGD 189 becomes SGD 206.01 monthly after GST.

Free trials (typically 14 days) allow you to test monday.com before committing. Monthly billing is available but carries a 20% premium over annual pricing.

Core Features Worth Noting

Visual Boards & Automation

monday.com's drag-and-drop board interface is its strongest asset. Teams can create custom workflows with status columns (e.g., To Do → In Progress → Done), colour-code tasks by priority, and assign work instantly. Automation rules—such as "move task to Done when all sub-tasks are complete"—save substantial admin time.

Timeline & Gantt Views

For teams planning sprints or campaigns, Gantt charts help visualize dependencies and deadlines. This is essential for coordinating across departments, especially in larger Singapore operations.

Integrations with Local Tools

monday.com connects with Slack (widely used in Singapore tech hubs), Microsoft Teams, Google Drive, Zapier, and over 1,000+ apps. If your team uses DBS or OCBC banking APIs for payroll or expense tracking, you can build custom workflows via Zapier.

Reporting & Analytics

Built-in dashboards let you track project progress, team capacity, and bottlenecks. Useful for monthly reporting to stakeholders or investor updates required by Singapore's acra compliance framework.

Is monday.com Right for Your Singapore Team?

Best For:

  • Marketing & Creative Teams: Campaign planning, asset management, deadline tracking
  • Operations Teams: Process tracking, task assignment, cross-functional coordination
  • Tech & Product Teams: Sprint planning, roadmaps, bug tracking (with custom workflows)
  • Consulting & Agency Teams: Client project management, resource allocation, time logging
  • Teams of 5–50 people: Moderate team sizes where per-user cost is manageable

Not Ideal For:

  • Micro-teams (1–3 people) where cost outweighs benefit
  • Teams needing advanced financial management or CPF payroll integration
  • Organizations requiring extensive compliance audit trails (regulated sectors)
  • Teams heavily focused on resource or capacity planning across 50+ staff

Implementation & Support in Singapore

monday.com offers onboarding templates and video tutorials for free-tier users. Premium plans include access to success managers, though response times can extend 24–48 hours for Singapore-based SME customers.

The platform doesn't require integration with Singapore's local accounting software (e.g., CloudCounting or Xero) directly, but Zapier automation can bridge gaps. For teams using accounting software for GST reporting to ACRA, consider whether you'll need custom workflows.

Data, Security & Compliance

monday.com servers are hosted on AWS globally, not specifically in Singapore. For SMEs handling sensitive customer data under Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA), confirm that monday.com's standard data processing agreement (DPA) meets your compliance requirements. The platform does offer PDPA-compliant configurations, but it's worth requesting their legal documentation before signing.

Final Thoughts

monday.com is a strong, modern project management platform that delivers real value to Singapore SMEs managing cross-functional work. Its intuitive interface, powerful automation, and visual workflows justify the cost for teams of 10–30 people. However, the per-user pricing (especially after GST) means smaller teams should explore free or cheaper alternatives like Notion or Asana's lower tiers first.
